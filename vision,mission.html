OUR STORY

Swach hospital was established in 2010 by a team of India’s leading neurologists in Nellore, Swach started its journey as a 100-beded neurology Institute with a core team of 25 neurologists, 2 operating theatre and 1 neurology laboratory. 10 years later, the Swach Hospitals Group is a multi-specialty healthcare provider with 4 hospitals serving 4 cities across 4 states of India.

Branches serving are in Coimbatore, Nellore, New Delhi, Vadodara. New Delhi being the headquarters, there are 300 beds (including 30 ICU beds), 7 operating theatres with fully equipped advanced modern equipments.Swach Hospitals delivers comprehensive care in over 5 specialties -- Neurology, Cardiology, Orthopedics, ENT, General Surgery.There are more than 1000 employees,100 specialized doctors, 20 assistant department heads, 4 department heads including all the 4 branches.

Adopting a service-oriented delivery model, Swach Hospitals provides cost-effective medical care, with an uncompromising commitment to its core purpose – ‘To make quality healthcare accessible at affordable costs to all. The hospital is based on a foundation of Empathy which translates into a lifelong commitment to serving the community. Constantly pushing our horizons to excellence, we are continuously seeking solutions to provide better patient care by improving our overall facilities of hospital management and at the same time ensuring control on the patient cost.
